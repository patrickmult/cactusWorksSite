About Me & What I Do

I am currently a freelance web developer specializing in websites for small businesses. I graduated from Chico State with a degree in economics, which gives me a perspective as a web developer that has been useful in working with business owners and understanding their goals and needs. I built my first website back in 2001 on a 56k modem. Back then the internet was a very different place.

Computers are becoming a minority device for browsing the web in place of smartphones and tablets. I design my work with responsive elements so it can be seen at it's best from both desktop and smaller mobile screens. Please don't hesitate to get in touch with me for web development, or really any other reason. I enjoy talking with other people about their projects and ideas as much as I enjoy working on code.
